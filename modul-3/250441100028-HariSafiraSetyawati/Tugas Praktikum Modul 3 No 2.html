About Us

True to Skin was born from a deeply personal journey by Riska Elastria. Marked by frustration, flare-ups, and countless failed skincare experiments. Our founder has struggled with extremely sensitive skin for most of her life. What seemed like a simple goal—finding products that truly worked without causing irritation—turned into a constant cycle of trial, error, and disappointment. In a world overflowing with harsh actives and one-size-fits-all solutions, she realized what was missing: skincare that puts sensitivity first without compromising on results.
